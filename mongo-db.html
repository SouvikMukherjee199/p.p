MongoDB

MongoDB continues to innovate and enhance its offerings, with several notable updates in 2024. One significant development is the introduction of DirectQuery support for the MongoDB Atlas Power BI Connector. This feature allows Power BI to query data in real-time directly from MongoDB Atlas, ensuring up-to-date data visualization without the need for data importation​ (MongoDB)​. Additionally, MongoDB 7.0 has brought various improvements, including enhanced aggregation operations, better security features, and improved time series capabilities​ (MongoDB)​. MongoDB is also expanding its capabilities in AI with the launch of Atlas Vector Search, integrated with Amazon Bedrock, and a collaboration with Google to enhance their Gemini Code Assist AI tool for MongoDB​ (SiliconANGLE)​.
